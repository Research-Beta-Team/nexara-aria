# NEXARA — Cost: 1-Month MVP (Vibe Coding, 2 Devs) + 1-Month Demo (20 Potential Customers)

**Version:** 1.0 (Model)  
**Audience:** Board, Finance, Founders  

This document estimates cost for: **(1)** building the MVP in 1 month with 2 developers using vibe coding, and **(2)** running a 1-month demo with 20 potential customers hitting the product.

---

## 1. Timeline

| Phase | Duration | Goal |
|-------|----------|------|
| **Month 1** | 1 month | MVP built (vibe coding, 2 developers) |
| **Month 2** | 1 month | Demo to 20 potential customers (they use/hit the product) |

**Total:** 2 months from start to end of demo.

---

## 2. Month 1 — MVP Build (Vibe Coding, 2 Developers)

**Developer rate:** **$200–500/month** per developer.

**Assumptions:** 2 developers full-time (or equivalent); “vibe coding” = AI-assisted development (Cursor, Copilot, etc.) to ship faster; MVP = core NEXARA + ARIA demo, auth, main flows, deployable for demos.

| Category | What it includes | Monthly cost (USD) |
|----------|-------------------|---------------------|
| **Developers (2)** | 2 × $200–500/month | $400–1,000 |
| **Infra (dev)** | Repos, staging host (Vercel/Netlify), dev tools, optional ARIA API for build (low usage, e.g. &lt;$300) | $300–1,200 |
| **G&A** | Minimal (already incorporated); no dedicated legal/accounting this month | $0–1,000 |
| **Total Month 1** | | **~$700–3,200** |

**Rounded:** **~$0.7K–3.2K** for Month 1 (MVP ready by end of month).

---

## 3. Month 2 — Demo to 20 Potential Customers

**Developer rate:** Same **$200–500/month** per developer. Assume 1 or 2 devs for support.

**Assumptions:** 20 potential customers are given access and “hit” the product (log in, run demos, use ARIA). Not yet paying; this is a trial/demo period. You need a stable environment and light support.

| Category | What it includes | Monthly cost (USD) |
|----------|-------------------|---------------------|
| **Developers** | 1–2 devs for bug fixes, demo support, and small improvements. 1 × $200–500 or 2 × $200–500 | $200–1,000 |
| **Infra** | Production-like hosting, DB, Stripe (optional for demo), monitoring for ~20 users | $800–1,500 |
| **ARIA (Anthropic)** | 20 demo users; assume ~25–40% of full 20-customer usage (lighter than paying customers) | $1,500–2,800 |
| **Support / onboarding** | Light onboarding and demo coordination (founder or 1 person part-time) | $500–1,500 |
| **S&M / demo** | Demo scheduling, basic sales tools or travel | $0–2,000 |
| **G&A** | Minimal | $0–1,000 |
| **Total Month 2** | | **~$3,000–9,800** |

**Rounded:** **~$3K–10K** for Month 2 (20 potential customers hitting the product for 1 month).

---

## 4. Total Cost (2 Months)

| Phase | Low (USD) | High (USD) |
|-------|-----------|------------|
| **Month 1 — MVP build** | ~700 | ~3,200 |
| **Month 2 — Demo (20 potential customers)** | ~3,000 | ~9,800 |
| **Total** | **~$3,700** | **~$13,000** |

**Summary:** **~$3.7K–13K** total for the 2-month path (1 month MVP + 1 month demo to 20).  
**Midpoint:** **~$8K** for planning.

---

## 5. What Drives the Range

- **Month 1:** Developer monthly rate ($200 vs $500) and infra. Vibe coding does not change dev cost; it can reduce calendar time to MVP.
- **Month 2:** Number of devs (1 vs 2), monthly rate, ARIA usage of the 20 demos, and whether you add dedicated demo/sales time.

---

## 6. After the Demo

- If some of the 20 convert to paying customers, **COGS** (ARIA, support, infra share) apply per paying customer; see `COST_TO_SERVE_1_CUSTOMER.md`.
- If you extend the demo or add more prospects, scale ARIA and infra with user count; support cost depends on commitment level.

---

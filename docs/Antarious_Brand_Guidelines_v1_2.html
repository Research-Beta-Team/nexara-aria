01 · Foundation

Brand Story

Antarious is the GTM intelligence layer for ambitious B2B companies. We replace manual marketing teams with 13 specialised AI agents — from ICP research to closed deal. Our brand communicates precision, speed, and operator-grade competence. We never sound like AI. We sound like the best operator you've ever worked with.

Colour Direction — Option D, Charged Teal: Bloomberg meets Palantir. The #0EA5E9 teal signals precision, data intelligence, and enterprise speed. It reads confident rather than aggressive — the colour of a system that's always ahead of you.

Logo

The Antarious mark is a wordmark with an integrated orbital symbol — the dot preceding the name is not punctuation, it is a planet in orbit. The mark anchors the entire visual identity. Never alter its proportions or colour. The Charged Teal era does not recolour the logo — it colours everything around it.

12 · Expression

Voice & Tone

Antarious sounds like a confident, experienced GTM operator. Freya speaks directly and specifically — never hedging, never self-referencing as AI, never using jargon as a substitute for clarity.

✓ Sound like this
"Your Q2 Meta campaign is live. ROAS at 4.8x — I reallocated £800 from the underperforming ad set automatically."
Direct, specific, owns the action, no hedging. States what happened, what changed, what it means.
✕ Avoid this
"I have successfully utilised advanced AI capabilities to optimise your campaign performance metrics."
Passive voice, AI self-referencing, jargon. Freya never says "AI" in her own responses — she just does it.
RuleCorrectIncorrect
Identify as AI"I have updated your ICP.""As an AI, I have updated..."
Completions"Done. Your campaign is live.""Task completed successfully!"
NumbersAlways specific: "£4,200" "23 sequences"Vague: "a significant amount"
Headlines"Your marketing team, now autonomous.""Revolutionising marketing with AI."
Forbidden wordsRevolutionise · Cutting-edge · Leverage · Synergy · Harness · Next-gen · Empower
13 · Motion

Motion & Animation

Subtle, purposeful, never distracting. One orchestrated entrance per section — not scattered micro-animations everywhere. Motion should reinforce that Antarious is always working.

Page Reveal
opacity 0→1 + translateY 14px→0
All sections on scroll enter. Stagger children 80ms. Duration: 500ms ease.
Button Hover
translateY(−2px) + shadow
Primary & accent only. 220ms ease. Never rotate or scale buttons.
Card Hover
border-color + shadow + left bar
250ms ease. Left 4px teal bar opacity 0→1 on marketing cards.
Status Pulse
opacity 1→0.35→1
Agent status dots only. 1.8–2s infinite. Never on static indicators.
Input Focus
border-color + box-shadow ring
200ms ease. Teal ring marketing, Teal Light ring UI.
⚠️ Always respect prefers-reduced-motion: reduce. Wrap all animations in a media query check. All reveals should be instant when reduced motion is enabled.
14 · Accessibility

Accessibility

All Antarious interfaces must meet WCAG 2.1 AA minimum. Target AAA for primary text. Verify all Charged Teal colour pairings before shipping — teal is bright, contrast must be validated carefully.

#1C2B27
Ink on Cream #FAF8F3
~14.5:1
✓ AAA Pass
#0891B2
Teal Dark on Cream
~4.7:1
✓ AA Pass
Cream
White on Dark Void
~18:1
✓ AAA Pass
Teal
Teal Light on Dark Void
~7.8:1
✓ AA+ Pass

Note on Charged Teal on white: #0EA5E9 on pure white yields ~3.0:1 — below AA for body text. Always use #0891B2 (Teal Dark) for text on white backgrounds. #0EA5E9 is approved only for large text (18px+ or 14px bold+) and UI controls.

RequirementSpecification
Minimum contrast4.5:1 body (AA) · 3:1 large text (AA) · 7:1 target for primary text (AAA)
Focus indicatorsVisible focus ring on all interactive elements — Teal (marketing) / Teal Light (product)
Touch targetsMinimum 44×44px on all touch devices
Semantic HTMLProper heading hierarchy h1→h6. Never skip levels for visual sizing.
ARIAAll icon-only buttons need aria-label. Status dots need aria-hidden="true".
Colour aloneNever use colour as the only differentiator — always pair with label, icon, or pattern.

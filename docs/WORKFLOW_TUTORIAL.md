# NEXARA — Workflow Tutorial (Non-Technical)

**Audience:** End users, customer success, board (product overview)

---

## 1. What Is NEXARA?

NEXARA is an AI-powered workspace for marketing teams and agencies. It helps you run campaigns, manage content, see performance, and get recommendations from **ARIA** — an AI co-pilot that can answer questions, draft content, and suggest actions.

---

## 2. Getting Started

### Sign up or log in

- Go to **Sign up** (or **Log in** if you already have an account).
- Enter your **company name**, your name, work email, and password.
- After signing up, you’re taken to **onboarding**.

### Onboarding (first-time setup)

1. **Company type**  
   Choose what best describes you: solo/freelancer, startup, growing agency, or enterprise. NEXARA will suggest a plan.

2. **Choose your plan**  
   You’ll see a recommended plan (Starter, Growth, Scale, or Agency) with price and features. You can accept it or pick another.

3. **Connections (optional)**  
   You can connect your company website, CRM, or ad accounts. You can skip and add these later in Settings.

4. **Done**  
   - **Go to Dashboard** — start using NEXARA.  
   - **Create your first campaign with ARIA** — upload a brief or document; ARIA will help build a campaign from it.

### Creating your first campaign with ARIA

- If you chose **“Create your first campaign with ARIA”**, you’ll see a screen where you can:
  - **Drop a file** (brief, prospect list, strategy doc) — ARIA reads it and helps build a campaign.
  - Or **start from scratch**.
- After ARIA processes your file (or you start from scratch), you’ll see a short preview and can go to the **Campaign** screen or the **Dashboard**.

## 3. Main Areas of the App

### Dashboard

- Overview of campaigns, performance, and tasks.
- **New campaign** — start a campaign manually or with ARIA.
- **Start campaign from file** — upload a file from the dashboard; then ARIA uses it when creating the campaign.
- Widgets and links depend on your **plan** and **role** (e.g. Owner vs CSM vs SDR).

### Campaigns

- **Campaign list** — all campaigns; click one to open details.
- **Campaign detail** — tabs for Strategy, Plan, Content, Calendar, etc. You can edit the plan, manage content, and use ARIA to generate or adjust things.
- **New campaign** — either the full **wizard** (step-by-step) or **Create with ARIA** (guided by ARIA).

### ARIA Co-pilot

- **Floating button** (bottom right) — opens the ARIA panel.
- In the panel you can:
  - Type questions or use **quick action** chips.
  - Use **History** to switch between past chats or **project folders**.
  - **Attach files** and use **voice input** (where supported).
- ARIA can explain data, suggest actions, and draft content. A **disclaimer** at the bottom reminds you to verify important decisions and data.

### Other areas

- **Inbox** — messages and replies (plan-dependent).
- **Content Library** — content pieces and approval status.
- **Analytics / Meta Monitor** — performance and alerts.
- **Revenue** — Pipeline, Customer Success, Forecast (plan-dependent).
- **Settings** — profile, plan, credits, ARIA persona, connections.
- **Billing / Upgrade** — change plan or buy more credits.

---

## 4. How Different Users See NEXARA

- **Owner/CEO** — full view; decisions, campaign risk, weekly brief.
- **Founder** — streamlined nav; same high-level focus.
- **CSM** — client-focused: briefs, reports, check-ins.
- **Media buyer** — ads, spend, audiences, performance.
- **Content strategist** — content calendar, reviews, brand.
- **SDR** — inbox, replies, follow-ups, leads.
- **Analyst** — data, reports, read-only escalations.
- **Client** — minimal nav: dashboard, content, inbox, settings.

Same URL can show different menus and actions depending on **role**.

---

## 5. Plans and Credits

- Your **plan** (Starter, Growth, Scale, Agency) decides which features and limits you have.
- **Credits** are used for AI actions (e.g. generating content). You get a monthly allowance; you can buy more or upgrade the plan.
- If a feature is locked, you’ll see an **Upgrade** or **Unlock** option that takes you to Billing.

## 6. Quick Reference

| I want to…              | Where to go                    |
|-------------------------|---------------------------------|
| See everything at a glance | Dashboard                    |
| Start a new campaign     | Dashboard → New campaign, or Campaigns → New |

| Ask ARIA something      | Click the ARIA button (bottom right) |
| Check past ARIA chats    | ARIA panel → History            |
| Manage content          | Content Library or Campaign → Content tab |
| Change plan or credits  | Settings or Billing / Upgrade   |
| Connect website/CRM/ads | Settings → Connections          |

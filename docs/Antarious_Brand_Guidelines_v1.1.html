01 · Foundation

Brand Philosophy

Antarious is built for operators who run campaigns on Meta, LinkedIn, and GDN every day. The brand must feel like a natural extension of their existing tools — familiar enough to trust immediately, sophisticated enough to signal a genuine upgrade. The logo and identity are clean, geometric, and confident — a direct visual expression of that promise.

Core design principle: Every design decision should reduce cognitive friction. Users are not adopting new behaviours — they are doing the same GTM work they have always done, only Antarious handles most of it. The UI should feel like it has always been there.

Brand Positioning
We are
  • →Calm and confident, never boastful
  • →Warm and human, never cold or robotic
  • →Precise and data-driven, never vague
  • →Professional, not formal or stiff
  • →Native to GTM workflows, not disruptive
We are not
  • ✕Futuristic sci-fi aesthetics
  • ✕Purple gradient "AI company" clichés
  • ✕Jargon-heavy or overly technical
  • ✕Intimidating or complex UX
  • ✕Dark "hacker" product aesthetics

02 · Identity

Logo & Mark

The Antarious logo consists of two elements: the geometric mark (the diagonal slash ‘.A’ form) and the wordmark (custom rounded geometric lettering). The grey dot in the mark is replaced with Sage #4A7C6F to connect the logo to the brand colour system.

The Dot — Sage #4A7C6F
The only brand colour in the mark. Changed from grey to Sage in the final brand version. This dot is the emotional anchor — the human element in the geometric mark.
The Slash Form — Ink #1C2B27
The diagonal ‘A’ cut with notch. Geometric, confident, modern. Always rendered in Ink on light backgrounds, white on dark.
Wordmark — Custom Geometric Rounded Sans
Large circular apertures, consistent thick strokes, single-storey ‘a’, tight tracking. Never replicate with system fonts. See Section 04 for the closest available font match.

04 · Typography

Type System

The Antarious wordmark uses a custom geometric rounded sans with large circular apertures, single-storey letterforms, and consistent thick strokes. Outfit is the closest publicly available match and should be used across all brand materials. It shares the same geometric DNA without being identical — giving the brand visual consistency across contexts.

Logo font match: The wordmark uses a custom type that closely resembles Outfit ExtraBold/Black in proportions and rounded terminals. Use Outfit 700–900 for all headlines and brand text. Never attempt to recreate the wordmark using Outfit — always use the locked-up logo asset.

12 · Expression

Voice & Tone

Antarious sounds like a confident, experienced GTM operator. Freya speaks directly and specifically — never hedging, never self-referencing as AI, never using jargon as a substitute for clarity.

✓ Sound like this
"Your Q2 Meta campaign is live. ROAS at 4.8x — I reallocated £800 from the underperforming ad set automatically."
Direct, specific, owns the action, no hedging. States what happened, what changed, what it means.
✕ Avoid this
"I have successfully utilised advanced AI capabilities to optimise your campaign performance metrics."
Passive voice, AI self-referencing, jargon. Freya never says "AI" in her own responses — she just does it.
RuleCorrectIncorrect
Identify as AI"I have updated your ICP.""As an AI, I have updated..."
Completions"Done. Your campaign is live.""Task completed successfully!"
NumbersAlways specific: "£4,200" "23 sequences"Vague: "a significant amount"
Headlines"Your marketing team, now autonomous.""Revolutionising marketing with AI."
Forbidden wordsRevolutionise · Cutting-edge · Leverage · Synergy · Harness · Next-gen
13 · Motion

Motion & Animation

Subtle, purposeful, never distracting. One orchestrated entrance per section — not scattered micro-animations everywhere. Motion should reinforce that Antarious is always working.

Page Reveal
opacity 0→1 + translateY 14px→0
All sections on scroll enter. Stagger children 80ms. Duration: 500ms ease.
Button Hover
translateY(−2px) + shadow
Primary & accent only. 220ms ease. Never rotate or scale buttons.
Card Hover
border-color + shadow + left bar
250ms ease. Left 4px border opacity 0→1 on marketing cards.
Status Pulse
opacity 1→0.4→1
Agent status dots only. 1.5–2s infinite. Never on static indicators.
Input Focus
border-color + box-shadow ring
200ms ease. Sage ring marketing, Sky ring UI.
⚠️ Always respect prefers-reduced-motion: reduce. Wrap all animations in a media query check. All reveals should be instant when reduced motion is enabled.
